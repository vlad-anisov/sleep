Sign in with Apple

How cool Would that be, if you could access your odoo accounts with your apple id? You won't need to learn the complex passwords everytime and also the security provided by apple is remarkable. Well, we have got you covered. Sign in with Apple module will simply let you to connect odoo with your apple id. With just one click you can log in to your odoo account with your apple credentials.

How to set up Apple credentials ?

The configurations file can be accessed from this link mentioned here : https://www.kanakinfosystems.com/download/appleauth

Sign in with Apple in odoo:-

Navigate to settings (make sure to turn on the developers mode) > Users & Companies > Click on OAuth Providers

Click on the "Sign in with Apple"

Enter your Apple ID credentials (Client ID, Team ID, Key ID and Key File(.p8 Certificate) ) and mark allowed true, Save the changes in Record.

On the Login page you will see the button to use apple credentials

Enter your apple credentials

You will be asked for Two-factor Authentication provided by Apple(If enabled).

After that you will be able to access your Odoo with your Apple credentials

Key Features

Easy to setup with the Apple credentials.
